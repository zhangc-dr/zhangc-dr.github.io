##### 2.窃听者为非合作的用户时其CSI完全无法获取吗？
最近在阅读 [Sensing-aided Near-Field Secure Communications with Mobile Eavesdroppers, TWC, 2025](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2408.13829) 。文中模型为ISAC基站服务于通信用户和感知目标，但是感知目标为窃听者且非合作，文中提到由于窃听者的非合作性导致无法获取其CSI，然后利用sensing功能来获取窃听者的位置信息构建信道。
的确，Los信道只和基站-窃听者的相对位置有关，那么就可以从位置信道中重构出Los信道；但是我想不明白的是，作者将基站到窃听者的信道建模为Rician信道，既然信道不可知，那么Rician信道中NLos部分完全无法获取，而且Rician factor也无法获取，出于这样的考虑，我对文章的严谨性持怀疑态度。

关键词：非合作，地面窃听者，Rician model

同样，[Reconfigurable Intelligent Surface Aided Physical-Layer Security Enhancement in Integrated Sensing and Communication Systems](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10306322) 这篇文章也考虑一个不合作的窃听者，无法获取其信道信息。

> 原文：As the EVE is illicit and non-cooperative to the ISAC system, the RIS-EVE channel is assumed to be unknown and unavailable in this paper.

[Robust and Secure Resource Allocation for ISAC Systems: A Novel Optimization Framework for Variable-Length Snapshots, TC, 2022](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2206.13307), 这篇文章同样考虑了窃听者信道无法被获取而且建模为Rician信道。
> 原文：Furthermore, potential eavesdroppers usually conceal themselves and do not proactively interact with the DFRC BS for CSI estimation. As a result, the DFRC BS may not be able to acquire information regarding the multi-path fading component of the eavesdroppers’ channels. Hence, in this paper, we assume that information regarding the multi-path fading component of the channels between the DFRC BS and the potential eavesdroppers is not available.
